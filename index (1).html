About Me

Hello, my name is Arnav Pagawad. I am a second-year film student at a reputed college in Mumbai, passionate about acting, direction, and writing. Over the past year, I have actively performed in theatre, honing my skills as an actor while simultaneously exploring storytelling through film..

As a filmmaker, I have written and directed two short films and a documentary as part of my student projects. One of my short films was recognized at the Darjeeling Film Festival, securing the award for the 4th Best Short Film. My approach to filmmaking is deeply rooted in exploring unique narratives, experimenting with visual storytelling, and creating immersive cinematic experiences..

I am constantly seeking opportunities to grow as an artist , whether in front of the camera or behind it.
